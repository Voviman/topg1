Our Services

SERVICES WE OFFER

Truckload Services

Truckload services are offered to customers that manufacture their products in the U.S. or abroad and imports by sea or air.


Truckload services usually imply shipments of 10,000 to 45,000 lb.

LTL, Less Than Truckload

LTL, or Less Than Truckload services are offered to customers that manufacture parts, supplies and other products in the U.S. or abroad and import by sea or air.


LTL services usually imply shipments of less than 10,000 lb

Dedicated Services

Dedicated services are normally offered to Distributors/Brokers of products used in day-to-day public consumption. This includes both perishable and non-perishable products.

Open Deck Shipments

Sometimes you customers may need to transport their farm tractor or some sort of special machinery which doesnâ€™t quite fit into a standard dry-van box.


These types of shipments require open deck trailers (step decks, or flatbeds) that may carry freight of up to 80,000 or more when needed, under special permitting.

Refrigerated Products

Meat producers, ice-cream companies, milk distributors, and all manufacturers or distributors of perishable products - all require refrigerated equipment to distribute or deliver their products to stores.


Refrigerated shipments at Top G Cargo are handled at ease.

Warehousing & Distribution

Top G Cargo operates a number of warehousing terminals nationwide and our capacity allows for short, medium or long-term storage of both dry and refrigerated products


Whenever, or wherever you need a place to store your product - do give us a call!
